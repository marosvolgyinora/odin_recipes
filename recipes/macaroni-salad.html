Summer Macaroni Salad

Description

What is Summer Macaroni Salad? Itâ€™s macaroni salad but with a fun twist. The secret lies in the dressing. Unlike regular macaroni salad which uses Miracle Whip, Amish macaroni salad uses real mayo, yellow mustard, sugar, and vinegar, creating a sweet and tangy flavor.

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni (uncooked)
  • 3 hard-cooked eggs (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 3 celery stalks (chopped)
  • 1 small red pepper (seeded and chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons dill pickle relish
  • 2 cups creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip)
  • 3 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon celery seed
  1. Cook macaroni noodles according to package instructions. Set aside to cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the hard-boiled eggs, onion, celery, red pepper, and dill pickle relish.
  3. In a separate container, mix together the salad dressing, mustard, white sugar, vinegar, salt and celery seed.
  4. Pour the dressing mixture over the vegetables. Add the macaroni and mix until well combined.
  5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least one hour. Serve chilled.
